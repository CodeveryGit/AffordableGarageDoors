Sectional garage doors are a popular choice because they are durable, functional and comparatively cheaper than other types of garage door. They are constructed with hinged panels. When the door is opened, the panels slide onto the metal track and are transported along the roof of the garage. They are a great choice for any garage, particularly if space is at a premium.
