ROLLER DOORS
Roller doors are extremely space efficient. The door, usually made out of thin material, wraps around a cylinder when it opens. It takes up very little ceiling room compare to the sectional door and is ideal for people who don’t have a lot of room in their garage. A popular choice for many Australian homes, the roller door is constructed of corrugated steel and it is these corrugations that provide the door with strength against impacts. Get a roller door installed in your home or business.
